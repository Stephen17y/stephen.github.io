Patriotic Parties in Ghana

There are 29 political Parties in Ghana and a few are listed below;

  • New Patriotic Party (NPP)
  • National Democratic Congress (NDC)
  • All People's Congress (ACP)

The New Patriotic Party, NPP

The NPP is one of the polical parties which in the year 1992, in Ghana was established. It has been one of the dominating parties since the year 1992 because of the great works performed by the leaders and the members in the party. The NPP is the current ruling party in Ghana since the 2018 elections. This party has really developed Ghana and keeps on doing so. The leader of NPP is William Addo Dankwa Akufo-Addo:
